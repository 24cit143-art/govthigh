Student Projects

Below are selected projects completed by our students. Each entry includes objectives, a short description, and the outcome.

Science Fair: Renewable Energy Model

Class: 9th   |   Date: March 2025

Objective: Understand solar and wind energy and build a working model.

Description: Students designed a combined solar-wind powered generator model demonstrating energy conversion and storage. They presented findings on efficiency and cost.

Outcome: Model won 1st place in the district science fair; report and photos available on request.

History Project: Local Heritage Documentation

Class: 10th   |   Date: January 2025

Objective: Research and document historical sites in the town.

Description: Groups visited local landmarks, interviewed elders, and compiled a booklet with photos and stories to preserve heritage.

Outcome: Booklet published for the school library and a short presentation held for parents.

Computer Science: Simple Website for Community Center

Class: 11th   |   Date: September 2025

Objective: Teach basic HTML & teamwork by building a functional website.

Description: Students created a multi-page site with information, contact form (non-functional in this demo), and event gallery for the local community center.

Outcome: Live demo presented to the center; source code stored in the school repository.
